The Brand History

2005

REACTION COLLECTION PRODUCTION

The release of the REACTION COLLECTION which was first worn by Abdulmaleek Adams on the 5th February,2005. Over 10 million naira was made from this collection and was not only known in Africa but Wordlwide,which made Fashion AWARDS nominate the brand for the URBAN LUXE AWARD and was awarded to the brand.

2008

COLLABORATION WITH NIKE

The brand made THE NIGERIAN EAGELES JERSEY which the brand brought in Nike to work with her. The JERSEY is the best release till date.
